Section 01

What DataBridge Ops Provides

DataBridge Ops configures an automated ops intelligence system ("the System") built on your existing spreadsheet data. The System includes up to three layers as scoped during intake:

L1
Automated daily/shift productivity reporting
Structured reports generated and pushed to Slack, Microsoft Teams, Google Chat, or email automatically at configured intervals.
L2
Executive reporting automation
Monthly and quarterly summaries auto-generated on scheduled triggers, formatted for your exec audience.
L3
On-demand operational intelligence
A plain-language query interface that answers operational questions from your team's data in real time.

Exact scope is confirmed during the data audit phase (Days 1–3 of the build) and documented in your Notion client portal.
